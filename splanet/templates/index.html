About Secure Planet

We are a leading provider of CCTV, solar, office, and home automation solutions in Kannur, Kerala. We offer a wide range of products and services to our customers, including installation, maintenance, repairs, and upgrades. Our team of experts is always on hand to provide you with the best possible service, and we pride ourselves on our high customer satisfaction rates.
